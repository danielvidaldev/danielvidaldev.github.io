ABOUT

WEB DEVELOPER

Despite being "tech-savvy" since my early teen years, it was not until I worked on my own startup when I became truly interested in web development. That is when, as a project manager, I decided to get my official education as a Product Owner and Scrum Master. But then I realized that I actually wanted to "create" websites myself, and began to learn web development as a self-taught student. After some websites fully designed and coded by myself, I feel very confident on HTML, CSS and Bootstrap, I am working on improving my Javascript, MongoDB and Node.js, and have as next objectives to learn React, Vue.js and Python. As a junior developer, I am highly motivated to keep improving and learning these skills!

HOSPITALITY AND PROJECT MANAGER

I have several years of experience managing teams of up to 25 people in very diverse fields: HR, Finance, Operations, Inventory Management, Software Implementations, Account Management, etc.  As well, I launched a startup as a solo founder, from developing the first drafts of the initial idea until being fully operational, managing all aspects of such a big operation. In other words: I understand your needs while developing a project. As "I have been there" myself.

MYSELF

Naturally from southern Spain, and graduated in International History from the University of Barcelona (UB). I am an urban traveller, passionate about 60s, 70s and 80s music, a nostalgic of times never lived, an avid reader of Charles Bukowski and Karl Ove Knausgard, amongst others. I like to think I am a mix of the places I have lived in and the people I have met along the way. And so it is my way of working, easy going, honest, down to earth, direct but respectful, never "overselling it" and a good communicator.
