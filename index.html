ABOUT

WEB DEVELOPER

Despite being "tech-savvy" since my early teen years, it was not until I worked on my own startup when I became truly interested in web development. That is when, as a project manager, I decided to get my official education as a Product Owner and Scrum Master. But then, I realized that what I actually wanted was to "create websites" myself, and decided to learn web development as a self-taught student. After some websites fully designed and coded by myself, I feel very confident on HTML, CSS and Bootstrap, I am working on improving my Javascript, MongoDB and Node.js, and have as next objectives to learn React, Vue.js and Python. As a Junior Developer, I am highly motivated to keep improving these skills and to learn new ones!

HOSPITALITY AND PROJECT MANAGER

I have several years of experience managing teams of up to 25 people in very diverse fields: HR, Finance, Operations, Inventory Management, Software Implementations, Account Management, etc.  As well, I launched a startup as a solo founder, from developing the first drafts of the initial idea until being fully operational. Managing all aspects of such a big operation. In other words: I truly understand your needs while developing a project, as "I have been there" myself.

MYSELF

Based in Amsterdam. Originally from southern Spain. Graduated in International History from the University of Barcelona (UB). I am an urban traveller, passionate about good movies, history, and 60s / 70s / 80s music. A nostalgic of times never lived, an avid reader of C. Bukowski and K. O. Knausgard, amongst others. I like to think I am a mix of the places I have lived in and the people I have met along the way. And so it is my way of working, easy going, honest, down to earth, direct but respectful, never "overselling it" and a good communicator.
